Natalie Montenegro, LMFT, MBA.

Natalie, founder and CEO of Natalie Montenegro Marriage and Family Therapy, Inc. (DBA: MontenegroMFT), is a bilingual (English/Spanish), licensed marriage and family therapist that has been involved in the mental health field since 2007. 

Natalie completed her undergraduate work at the University of Redlands and worked for the AVUHSD as a substitute teacher while she completed her Master's in Psychology, emphasizing in Marriage and Family Therapy at Phillips Graduate University. After graduating, Natalie immersed herself in community mental health working with various populations including adoption/foster families, families struggling with addiction and trauma, families struggling with acculturation difficulties, families struggling with relational difficulties such as divorce, and families with children suffering from co-occurring mental health conditions such as intellectual disabilities and autism.

In 2015, Natalie returned to school and completed her MBA from Brandman University. Natalie was raised in the Antelope Valley and takes pride in being able to offer hope to her community.
